About Me

I start each day with a simple goal. To end that day with more knowledge, or more physical abilty. If I can end each day with a little bit of both I have had a good day. Given this very simple goal my days can be very productive and engaging.

My professional goals are to find efficiencies in all things as to make them easier to use and longer lasting. The motivation behind this is that I want to be able to protect the environment and by creating quality processes products and designs the energy expended from the end users can minimise thier carbon footprint.

Life is rarely simple, we all have so much time to spend doing the things we love with those we love. Why not design everything with these ideas in mind?
